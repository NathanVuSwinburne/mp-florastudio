13 / Investment decision

Final recommendation

Build and launch the paid product; do not underwrite venture-scale acquisition yet. The architecture can sustain software-like gross margins because most checks stay on-device. Run a 90-day pricing/retention proof with annual Plus as the primary offer. Release growth budget only after two cohorts achieve ≥4.5% free-to-paid conversion, ≤4.5% monthly paid churn, CAC payback under six months, and diagnosis usefulness above 80%.

CFO view: a credible bootstrapped/profitable B2C product. VC view: investable at seed only if retention proves FloraStudio becomes a recurring care habit rather than a one-off plant diagnostic.
